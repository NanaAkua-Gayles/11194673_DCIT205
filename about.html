Hi!

This is the CS Department ...

Welcome to the Computer Science Department, a thriving center of innovation,
knowledge and collaboration as a pivotal force behind technological progress,
computer science forms the foundation of our ever-evolving digital world.
Here, we proudly showcase the remarkable potential and accomplishments of
our department, dedicated to nurturing brilliant minds, conducting ground breaking
research and preparing the next generation of trailblazers in this dynamic field.






Academic Excellence

In our department, we are committed to cultivating a culture of educational excellence
Our accomplished faculty members are renowned experts in their respective fields.

Enriching Opportunities

Our department gives a range of extracurricular activities which fosters creativity
and team work and as well as leadership skills. Here, we also encourage internships
and industry collaboration which helps our students gain real-world experience.

Creating Supportive Community

At the heart of our department is a strong sense of community and collaboration.
We create a welcoming and exclusive environment where our students come from a
different background and unite as one.
